Princess vs Dragon: Rescue Mission

Game Overview

Princess vs Dragon: Rescue Mission is a 2D fantasy action adventure game. The story begins when an evil dragon kidnaps the princess's childhood friend and imprisons him in a magic tower. To prevent anyone from rescuing the captive, the dragon has summoned four powerful monsters to guard every floor of the tower.

Gameplay

Players will take on the role of the brave princess and embark on a rescue journey. You need to clear four levels in sequence and defeat all four monster guardians:

1. Shadow Wolf: A swift beast lurking in the dark forest at the entrance of the tower.

2. Rock Golem: A sturdy giant that blocks the stone gate with brute force.

3. Poison Viper Spirit: A sly monster that attacks with deadly poison.

4. Fire Bat King: The fiercest guardian who rules the upper floor of the tower.

After defeating all four guardians, you will face the final boss — the giant black dragon. Use your skills and courage to defeat the dragon, break the curse of the magic tower, and finally rescue the childhood friend.

Game Features

• Classic level-based combat gameplay

• Four unique monster enemies with different abilities

• Epic final boss battle against the dragon

• Simple controls and interesting adventure storyline
